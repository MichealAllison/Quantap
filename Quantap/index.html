About Us

The FULAFIA Nanotechnology Platform offers scientific and technological support that includes the design, development, and analysis of devices, materials, and processes, so that academic researchers and companies alike may use the platform to develop their innovative ideas. The objective of the Platform is to establish a nationwide network providing assess to advanced facilities for nanotechnology. We aim to facilitate advanced research by providing services in the fields of quantum and nanotechnology for all academic and industrial researchers. Some of the many areas of application include nanoengineering, BioMEMS, materials science, tissue engineering, optic, and biomaterials. Our platform's work focuses on developing a science-based understanding of the physico chemical properties of these nanomaterials and their interactions with biological systems.
